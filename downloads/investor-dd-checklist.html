PickleOps Investor Due Diligence Checklist

Product

  • Clear MVP scope
  • Pilot usage evidence
  • Roadmap discipline

Market

  • ICP validation interviews
  • Competitor positioning
  • Willingness-to-pay proof

Business

  • Pricing model and unit economics
  • Pilot-to-paid conversion funnel
  • Churn and retention targets

Technical

  • Security and permissions model
  • Audit logs and reliability strategy
  • Scalability plan
