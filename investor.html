Why Now / Market Gap

  • Fragmented stack: communities still juggle chat, scheduling, scoring, and ratings tools.
  • League workflows remain manual in many clubs (sheets/paper fallback).
  • Operator pain persists: bugs, weak scheduling flows, low-trust score/standings process.
